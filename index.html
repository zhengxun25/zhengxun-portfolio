02 — Backtesting / Risk-to-Reward / Trading Analytics

Expectancy Analysis

Backtesting Risk-to-Reward Trading Analytics

This project explores how different combinations of win rate and risk-to-reward ratio can still lead to profitability. The results show that I do not need a high win rate to succeed, as effective risk management and favourable RR ratios can compensate for lower accuracy.

The project also includes a day-of-week performance analysis, allowing me to identify which days historically perform best or worst for my strategy.

By analysing performance across different trading days, I can make more informed decisions and continuously refine my approach based on historical data.

03 — Portfolio / Passive Investing

Passive Portfolio — Robo-Advisory Allocation

Portfolio Passive Investing ETFs

I chose Syfe Core Growth because it provides broad diversification while keeping risk at a manageable level. Instead of investing entirely in stocks, the portfolio is spread across equities, bonds, and gold, allowing different asset classes to balance each other during periods of market volatility.

A key feature that stands out is Syfe’s automatic rebalancing. If one asset class grows too large relative to the target allocation, the portfolio is adjusted to maintain its intended risk level. This helps keep the portfolio aligned with my long-term objectives without requiring constant monitoring.

Syfe also uses a Smart Beta approach, which places greater emphasis on financially strong companies while reducing concentration in a few large stocks. As a long-term investor, I can automate my dollar-cost averaging (DCA) contributions, allowing me to invest consistently regardless of market conditions while benefiting from dividend reinvestment.

Compared to stock picking, this reduces company-specific risk, and compared to a 100% equity portfolio, it offers a smoother investment journey with lower downside risk. Overall, Core Growth aligns with my preference for steady, risk-adjusted returns and long-term wealth accumulation.
